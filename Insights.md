Page 1: Overview Insights

Runs by Season
Insight:

Total runs have generally increased over time, suggesting that modern IPL cricket has become more batting-friendly.
Recent seasons show higher scoring rates compared to earlier editions due to aggressive batting approaches and evolving match strategies.

Wins by Team
Insight:

A few franchises consistently rank among the top teams in total wins, highlighting strong squad management and long-term performance.
Teams with stable leadership and core player groups tend to perform better across multiple seasons.

KPI Insights
Insight:

The ratio of boundaries (fours and sixes) to total runs indicates that a substantial portion of IPL scoring comes through aggressive stroke play.
Growth in total matches over the years has expanded opportunities for players to accumulate runs and wickets.


Page 2: Batting Analysis Insights

Top Batsmen
Insight:

The leading run scorers combine consistency and longevity, performing across multiple IPL seasons.
A relatively small group of batters contributes a large share of total tournament runs.

Boundary Analysis
Insight:

Some batters rely more on fours, reflecting a traditional stroke-playing style.
Others score a greater proportion of runs through sixes, indicating power-hitting ability and higher impact during death overs.

Strike Rate Analysis
Insight:

Players with both high strike rates and high run totals provide the greatest value because they score quickly while maintaining consistency.
Extremely high strike rates alone do not guarantee high run aggregates if the player faces relatively few deliveries.


Page 3: Bowling Analysis Insights

Top Bowlers
Insight:

The most successful bowlers consistently take wickets across seasons rather than relying on a few exceptional performances.
Wicket-taking ability remains one of the strongest indicators of bowling effectiveness in T20 cricket.

Wickets by Season
Insight:

Seasons with more matches naturally produce higher wicket totals.
Variations in wickets per season may reflect changes in batting aggressiveness and playing conditions.

Bowling Performance
Insight:

Some bowlers achieve high wicket counts through longevity, while others maintain superior wicket-taking efficiency on a per-match basis.
Efficient bowlers often have a greater impact on match outcomes despite playing fewer matches.

Toss Impact
Insight:

Teams frequently choose to field first, indicating a preference for chasing targets under modern T20 conditions.
Toss decisions reflect tactical responses to pitch and weather conditions.
